Building a Fresh and Modern Brand Identity for Salad Club

Client: Salad Club
Industry: Food & Beverage
Services Provided: Logo Design, Brand Identity, Brand Guidelines

Project Overview

RestoreFine partnered with Salad Club to develop a strong and modern brand identity that reflects the brand’s focus on fresh, healthy food. As a digital marketing and branding agency in Glasgow, our goal was to create a distinctive logo and a complete brand guideline system that would help the business maintain a consistent visual identity across digital platforms, menus, packaging, and marketing materials.

Our team designed a clean and recognizable logo and produced a comprehensive brand guidelines document in PDF format. This ensured that the brand could maintain consistency in its visual communication across social media, promotional materials, and future marketing campaigns.

Challenges
When Salad Club approached RestoreFine, the brand was in its early stage and required a clear visual identity to establish its presence in the competitive food and restaurant market.

No Existing Brand Identity
The business was starting from scratch and did not have a logo or defined visual branding.

Need for Consistency Across Platforms
Without structured brand guidelines, it would be difficult to maintain a consistent look across menus, packaging, social media, and marketing materials.

Creating a Modern and Fresh Brand Image
The brand needed a visual identity that would communicate freshness, health, and quality while appealing to a modern audience.

Our Strategy
Brand Discovery
• Understood the brand vision, target audience, and positioning
• Identified key attributes such as freshness, health, and simplicity

Logo Design
• Designed a clean and modern logo that reflects the brand’s concept
• Ensured the logo works across digital and print applications

Brand Guidelines Development
• Created a comprehensive brand guideline document
• Defined typography, color palette, logo usage rules, and design elements
• Provided a full PDF guide to ensure consistency across all future branding materials
